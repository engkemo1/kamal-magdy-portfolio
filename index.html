Chapter 03 — The Crucible

Defining Moments

ODC FLUTTER HACKATHON #1

Overcoming the Impossible

In 2022, while balancing my final university exams, I saw a post on LinkedIn: The ODC Flutter Hackathon.

I entered a room full of experienced developers. The pressure was immense. How could I compete with so many pros? I remember telling myself: "Nothing is impossible. Believe in Allah and do your best."

01
The Course: Built a full Learning Management System in 4 days. Figma design, complex APIs, and Clean Architecture. I was proud to stay among the final 30 participants.
02
The Game: The final challenge was insane: "Build a game in 4 hours." Under heavy pressure, I planned, designed, and coded a Flutter Color Game. It was one of the best in the room.

Seven days later, I got the call: Top 5 in the Hackathon. This led to a 6-month journey working with ODC, proving that passion and hard work can conquer any obstacle.
